Ashtavinayak Temples - Locations & Tour Plan


The Ashtavinayak Yatra is a pilgrimage to eight famous Ganesh temples in Maharashtra, India. These temples are considered self-manifested (Swayambhu) and are dedicated to different forms of Lord Ganesha.
